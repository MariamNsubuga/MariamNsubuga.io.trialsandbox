WHITNEY HOUSTON

A lady who had a voice!

  • Whitney Elizabeth Houston was born in August 9, 1963.
  • Her parents were John Russell Houston and Cissy Houston.
  • Was Married to Bobby Brown.
  • Had only one child, Bobbi Kristina Brown.
  • Had her education at Mount Saint Dominic Academy.
  • She was a singer,actress,film producer and a model.
  • Died on February 11, 2012.
  • She lived at Bevery Hills, Califorina, U.S.
  • Cause of her death was due to coronary artery and drowning.
  • She was lied to rest at Fairview Cemetery Westfield, New Jersey,US.
